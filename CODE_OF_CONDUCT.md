# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (Short)

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors.

Our expectations
- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Avoid demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behavior.
- Be collaborative and open to feedback.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or reach out to the repository owners.

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.0.
